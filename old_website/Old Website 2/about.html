About
01

Before college, never did I wonder how drastic my life would change in such a short period of time. During my first few years at college I enlisted and contributed to a technology news site simply titled Zunited, now rebranded as The Redmond Post. The site revolved around a Microsoft product titled the Zune.

This under rated device sparked my love for design and technology. Never before had I seen such a contrasting device before.It was around the year 2010 when I first laid eyes on the devices and it would be no wonder that this device would be the one to change my life, indirectly the Zune HD changed design around the world causing the flat design boom. The evolution of the Zune would bring fourth the Windows Phone UI which heavily promoted flat design which would cause a paradigm shift within the tech world causing Apple and Google to follow suit and add flat elements within their respective operating systems.

This little device would convince me of the art world in the digital field. I now wanted to create art through technology and thus why I have a diverse love affair with Graphic Design, Computer Science, and Journalism.
