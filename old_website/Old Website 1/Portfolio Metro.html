Journalism

I have experience in the online world of journalism and speacialize in technology trends around the world. I have two full years of experience free lancing for the Microsoft focused news site The Redmond Post.

The Redmond Post was original titled Zunited, a website dedicated to the old Zune HD portable music player before evolving to an all around Microsoft focused website. At its peak, Zunited would attract over one thousand visits a month.

The owner of the website is a current Microsoft employee and the current Editor in Cheif, David Kimbal, is also a newly initiated Microsoft employee.

My title at The Redmond Post is "Author" and with that title comes the responsibility of delivering up to the minute news on the world involving Microsoft by writing weekly articles using the latest search engine optimization techniques. Articles on the site involve but are not limited to Skype, Xbox, Windows, Office, Azure, Bing, and Windows Phone.

Seeing the benefits that tech giants like Microsoft bring to people is such an amazing experience and being able to share that feeling to an audience is just as amazing.

Graphic Design

According to Wikipedia Graphic Design is "...the art of communication, stylizing, and problem-solving through the use of type, space and image." With Graphic Design one can express art through an unexpected source.

How facinating is it when you realize every font, every image, every small detail on any type of media has a meaning behind it. An example of this would be the website on your monitor right now.

Believe it or not, everything on this site was carefully considered and at times replaced and build upon through out a few years until the site felt as though it represented what I wanted it to be. From the font size, to the link colors, every thing was hand molded demonstrate art through an unconventional mean. With Graphic Design, and more specifically, Webs Design, I intend to show the world art through technology.
